Supply Chain Management

SCM deals with contact with suppliers

  • Improves supply chain visibility and inventory velocity
  • Links business parnters

Structure & Components of Supply Chains

  • Upsteam - Sourcing from external suppliers
  • Internal - Work inside the organization
  • Downstream - Distribution
  • Flows - Material, information, financial

Value Chain

  • Plan - Find needed supplies
  • Source - Find suppliers
  • Make - Schedule production with supplies
  • Deliver - Logistics
  • Return - Return defective products

SCM ISs are Interorganizational

  • Eliminates errors
  • Enables rebuy
  • No paper processing
  • Faster

Push vs Pull Model

Solutions to Supply Chain problems

  • Just-in-Time (JIT)
