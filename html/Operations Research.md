<h2 id="definition">Definition</h2>
<blockquote>

<li>Find the positive coefficients in the pivot column</li>
<li>Divide the right-hand side by the pivot column for both, find the lowest result</li>
<li>The row with the lowest result is your <strong>Pivot row</strong> and the variable corresponding to it the <strong>Leaving basic variable</strong></li>

<li>In all equations change the coefficient of the <em>leaving basic variable</em>, so that the coefficient of the <em>entering basic variable</em> is zero</li>
<li>The entering basic variable is now the basic variable</li>
